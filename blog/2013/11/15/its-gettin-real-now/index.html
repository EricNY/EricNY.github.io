I'ts Gettin' Real Now

Nov 15th, 2013

Yup, the title about sums it up. This is the end of week 9. Three more to go and then it’s interview time. My main goal is to have a web app that actually works well and looks good. Not that I have been slacking the whole time here but this means it’s up to me to buckle down and focus even more until the end of the course. So this is what my project looks like so far after working on it all week:

http://frozen-brushlands-6019.herokuapp.com/

It is a brewery finder.

My two immediate goals are a video background and to have the user’s dashboard actually do something. For the video background there is a JQuery plugin out there called BigVideo.js that I can use but it is notoriously difficult to implement and I hope it does not destroy too much of my time. We used it in another project and it took Julian two days to figure it out (he also forgot how to do it just now when I asked him, hahaha). But trust me it will be cool, I envision a background of beer bubbles floating behind my app. Pointless? Sure. Will it even look cool? Don’t know. Does it add any functionality to the site? Nope. Does it demonstrate a knowledge of Rails, Backbone, etc… Nah (well it does require knowlege of javascript and JQuery). It is taking away from my time honing my backend skills? Yup. But dammit I want my frickin’ bubbles!!

Next up is to add functionality to the user’s log in page. For now I’m thinking I need three tabs. First we put in a search field to browse breweries and see description of it, next there is a map of all the places you have been, third is a wishlist of places you want to go which can also act as a pubcrawl planner.

Any input is appreciated:)

-E
